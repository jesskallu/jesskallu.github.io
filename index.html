Gajrela Recipe

What is Gajrela?

Gajrela is a sweet carrot pudding with its origins in the northern part of India and Pakistan. It is made by cooking grated carrots in sugar and milk.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg carrots
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 cup milk powder
  • handful of almonds

    • Instructions:

    Step 1: Peel and wash the carrots. Then grate them up into a large bowl. This step is the most tedious.

    Step 2: Pour the grated carrot pieces into a large pot and fry them on a low gas, until they are dried out and all of the water has boiled off.

    Step 3: Add the 4 tablespoons of sugar to the pot and continue to fry. You can adjust the sugar to your liking.

    Step 4: When you can see the water setting off again, add the spoonful of ghee and continue to fry until the mixture is at your preferred consitency.

    Step 5: Turn off the gas and add the cup of milk powder. Mix well until it is fully combined.

    Step 6: Pour the mixture into a tray and flatten with a spatula.

    Step 7: Chop up the almonds and add them ontop, making sure to press them in also with your spatula. Now it is ready to serve.
